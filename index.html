5 Rules From People Who've Done This

  1. Build > Watch. Every 2 courses, build something. Projects are your proof.
  2. Don't go deep on math. AI Engineering is about integration, not research.
  3. Don't quit your job. This entire plan works alongside full-time work.
  4. Use AI at your current job NOW. Fastest way to get noticed and build real experience.
  5. DeepLearning.AI free courses are gold. Almost everyone recommends starting there.
